Technologists who love transit.
Transit enthusiasts who hack!

At Transit Techies NYC, speakers present transit-related projects. Presenters may be household hackers, data scientists, researchers, product developers, or you! All presentations will be technical and awesome.

This group is inspired by the legendary DC Transportation Techies run by Michael Schade.
